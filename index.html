Features

Everything you need to
stay motivated

🏅

Custom Rewards

Add any reward you want — your favorite meal, a movie night, a new purchase. Set the calorie cost and make it yours.

🔗

HealthKit Integration

Pulls live calorie data directly from Apple Health. Every workout you do on your Apple Watch counts automatically.

📈

History & Streaks

See every reward you've earned, your workout streaks, and weekly calorie trends in a beautiful timeline.

🔔

Smart Haptics

Get a satisfying haptic pulse when you hit a milestone — 25%, 50%, 75% — and a full celebration when you earn your reward.

👥

Challenge Friends

Race a friend to the same reward. First one to burn the calories wins — and maybe gets to pick where you eat.

🎨

Watch Complications

A glanceable complication on your watch face always shows your current reward and how close you are to earning it.
